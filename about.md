Hi, I'm **husobee**. This is where I write down whatever I happen to be
interested in and feel the need to express to the world — hence the name.

Most of what shows up here is software engineering:

- **Go** — web services, URL routing (I wrote [Vestigo][vestigo]), middleware,
  concurrency, and the occasional fight with the standard library.
- **Security & cryptography** — PGP, TLS, protecting personal information, and
  notes from a master's in information security.
- **Testing** — behavior-driven testing, coverage, and making documentation that
  can't drift from reality.
- **Distributed systems, devops & the network stack** — Docker, gRPC, DHTs,
  tcpdump, and debugging the strange stuff that only happens under load.

And every so often something that isn't code at all — flying small airplanes,
woodworking, or a good cocktail.
